Save Game Alert Test

Test Scenarios

1. Play the game and get a score

2. Let the game end (hit an obstacle)

3. Click "SAVE SCORE" button

4. Observe the alert behavior:

  • ✅ Success: Green alert with checkmark, shows score saved
  • ❌ Error: Red alert with X, shows error message
  • 🔔 Auto-dismiss: Alert disappears after 4 seconds
  • ❌ Manual dismiss: Click X to close immediately

Expected Alert Behavior

✅ Success Alert

Score Saved!

Your score of 1234 has been saved to leaderboard.

❌ Error Alert

Save Failed

There was an error saving your score. Please try again.
